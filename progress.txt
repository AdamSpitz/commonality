## Third-party secondary marketplace research (Jan 2026)

Researched whether Seaport, SudoSwap, Rarible, or Reservoir could replace our ERC1155SecondaryMarket.
Conclusion: keep our custom contract. Details written up in specs/legal.md.
Key reasons:
- Seaport/Reservoir use off-chain orderbooks (can't call from DelegatableNotes on-chain)
- SudoSwap is an AMM, not an orderbook — different mental model, would need architecture rethink
- Our contract is simple/generic enough that publishing it isn't a legal concern
- The real legal concerns are about the UI/descriptions, not the contracts

## publishDocument / fetchDocument helpers (Jan 2026)

Added IPFS publish/fetch glue for displayable documents in sdk/src/displayable-document.ts:
- `publishDocument(doc)`: validates, canonical-JSON-encodes, uploads to IPFS, returns CID. Canonical encoding ensures identical documents always get the same CID regardless of key order.
- `fetchDocument(cid)`: fetches from IPFS, validates as displayable document, returns typed `DisplayableDocument | null`.
- 9 new tests (72 total SDK tests): round-trip, deterministic CIDs, key-order invariance, invalid doc rejection, non-existent CID, invalid fetched content.
- Files changed: sdk/src/displayable-document.ts, sdk/src/displayable-document.test.ts
