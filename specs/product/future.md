# Future steps

After the [MVP](./mvp.md), what else might we want to do?

- **Gaming/scamming/abuse prevention** — Identify attack vectors and protections.
- **Marketing** — Dedicated planning for exposure and promotion.
  - Strategic outreach: approaching Turning Point USA, Daily Wire, Jordan Peterson, etc. with a working prototype.
- **Fiat bridges** — Credit card / Apple Pay / Google Pay onramp for non-crypto users. Full design in [specs/tech/bridges.md](../tech/bridges.md).
- **Explorer AI** — AI-assisted statement discovery and the conceptspace explorer experience. Full design in [specs/product/content.md](content.md) and [specs/tech/subsystems/conceptspace/explorer.md](../tech/subsystems/conceptspace/explorer.md).
- **Seed content** — Curated statements for real early users. See [specs/tech/subsystems/conceptspace/seed-content/README.md](../tech/subsystems/conceptspace/seed-content/README.md).
- **Per-contract token choice** — Currently all contracts use USDC; post-MVP, let each assurance contract choose its own settlement token.
- **Embedded wallet provisioning** — Make crypto invisible to non-crypto-native users.
- **Mutable Refs UI** — The SDK layer is done; building the UI is deferred.
- **AI skills** — Formal SKILL.md files for the assistant roles described in [ai-assistance.md](ai-assistance.md).
- **Generative testing** — Infrastructure is prepped; the generative test suite itself isn't written.
