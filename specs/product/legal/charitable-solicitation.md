# Charitable-solicitation law

"Donation" framing triggers state charitable-solicitation registries in the US, and California's AB 488 regulates "charitable fundraising platforms" specifically.

**Mitigation:** never imply recipients are charities or that contributions are tax-deductible (the [matching-funds doc](/specs/product/matching-funds.md) already gets this right); consider avoiding the bare word "donate" for non-charity recipients — "contribute"/"support" is safer.
