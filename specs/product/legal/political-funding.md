# Political money — CSM and Civility

Already partially flagged in [bridges.md](/specs/tech/bridges.md) (the Coinbase "political activities" disclosure). The deeper issue: if CSM-adjacent projects fund anything that looks like election advertising or advocacy, we hit campaign-finance regimes — in Canada, third-party rules under the Canada Elections Act; in the US, the foreign-national prohibition means *Adam's* money or in-kind support (sponsored gas for a political contribution?) touching a US campaign is itself a violation.

**What to do:** an explicit content policy excluding election-period campaign funding from the platform at launch, and keep the already-flagged Coinbase disclosure honest. Revisit the Coinbase political-activities disclosure before CSM money flows.
