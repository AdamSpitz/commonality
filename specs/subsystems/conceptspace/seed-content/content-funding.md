# Content funding seed content

Here's the [seed content](../seed-content.md) for the [noninflammatory content](../content-patterns/noninflammatory-content.md) pattern.

See also [here](../../content-funding/noninflammatory-content/seed-statements.md); what's the difference between these files?

Key design principle: **don't write centrist statements that nobody would actually sign.** Write statements that people from specific communities would sign because it reflects something they genuinely want. The implication attester then discovers the connections between them.

**Hub statement:** "Content that communicates political perspectives without antagonizing people who disagree deserves financial support."

**Bridge statements:** Statements that many people across the spectrum might sign.
- "I want the political content I consume to inform me, not inflame me."
- "Political ideas are more persuasive when they're presented without contempt for the people who currently disagree."
- "If someone holds views I disagree with, I'd rather read a version that makes their best case than a version designed to make me angry at them."

**Left-coded statements:** Things progressives would sign, expressed in terms natural to the left.
- "Progressive ideas are strong enough to be communicated without demonizing conservatives."
- "I want conservative people in my life to be able to engage with progressive content without feeling attacked."
- "Left-wing media that makes me feel righteous but alienates everyone who disagrees with me is not actually helping my cause."

**Right-coded statements:** Things conservatives would sign, expressed in terms natural to the right.
- "Conservative ideas deserve a hearing that isn't poisoned by liberal media framing them as bigotry."
- "I want to be able to share my political views with liberal friends and family without being written off as a bad person."
- "Right-wing media that just dunks on the left is entertaining but it's not convincing anyone who doesn't already agree."

**Expected implication structure:** The left-coded and right-coded statements each imply the bridge statements, which imply the hub statement — building an organic coalition around noninflammatory content.
