# Fundable projects explorer seed content

These are seed statements for the fundable-project explorer — entry points that route users toward specific categories of fundable projects.

Each statement is phrased as something a real person would sign. Implication links show how statements connect to top-level "I care about X" statements (which appear in [meta.md](./meta.md) and [hidden-majority.md](./hidden-majority.md)).

---

## Finding common ground / depolarization

These are the statements most directly aligned with Commonality's thesis. High-priority to include.

- "I am interested in furthering the cause of helping people find common ground across political divides."
- "I am interested in furthering the cause of content that explains one side's views to the other side charitably and accurately."
- "I am interested in furthering the cause of identifying where political opponents actually agree."
- "I am interested in furthering the cause of reducing tribal polarization in public discourse."
- "I am interested in furthering the cause of journalism that informs rather than inflames."

Implication links:
- All → "Neither the left nor the right has a monopoly on good ideas about how to organize society" (meta)
- All → [noninflammatory content seed statements](./content-funding.md)
