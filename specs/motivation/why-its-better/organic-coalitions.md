# Individualization -> natural coalitions

THIS IS WHY THE SYSTEM IS NAMED COMMONALITY.

Centralized legacy systems polarize; we predict that [individualized](./individualization.md) systems like this (letting commonality be discovered organically by analyzing the individual statements to find common ground) will lead to much more agreement/overlap.

That is, implication attestations let two disparate groups discover shared ground and fund overlapping projects, without either group needing to compromise on their specific wording or even be aware of each other upfront.
