The core problem of public goods (non-excludable, non-rivalrous) is the free-rider problem.

Another basic problem with any sort of collective action is that people are lazy.

Commonality has a solution to both of those:
  - assurance contracts
  - delegation system

And then beyond that there are a bunch of more-mundane problems: finding and vetting project-doers, etc. Those are basically just "a simple matter of organization", though Web 2.0-style techniques can help with those too (rather than simply having some centralized org do them).
