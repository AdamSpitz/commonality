# Commonality really benefits new orgs

For new and unknown charity orgs, Commonality brings not only [the benefits that it brings established orgs](./benefits-established-orgs.md) but also the big benefit of the users being able to say "I don't know who that charity is but at least they're using the standard known infrastructure so I don't need to worry about the rails being unpolished/buggy/corrupt."

(Assuming we do a good job, of course.)
