## Common Sense Majority

Aspects to include:
  - Giving a voice to the hidden/moderate/common-sense majority in the middle.
  - We've created a way to crowdfund noninflammatory social media content. If you want to fund it, you can fire-and-forget $X/month in a way that you can trust. If you want to write it, this pool of money is available.
  - AI is doing most of the legwork. All transparent, prompts all open to be read. Configure your own if you prefer.
  - We've also got AIs working on *finding* common ground between moderate people from "opposite sides".
  - Also just head count: "Here's how many people have supported this statement."
  - Can also crowdfund projects intended to further that cause. Again, all open.

Answers to anticipated questions:
  - "So you're some sort of fund?" No, we've created this system through which anybody can contribute to funding noninflammatory content.
  - "So you're some sort of blog or journal or other content-creator?" No, we've created this system through which normal social-media content on X or YouTube or Substack or wherever can get funded from the crowd of people who are watching for this kind of content.
  - "What makes it noninflammatory?" Ultimately you decide - or your chosen person to whom you've delegated your funding decisions. But there are AIs to do the legwork, so mostly it's a matter of you (or your chosen delegate) just reading this thing that popped up on the feed of noninflammatory content and clicking "yup, I'll put a buck towards that one as long as enough other people do."
