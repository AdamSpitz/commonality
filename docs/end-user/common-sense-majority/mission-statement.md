# CSM mission statement

The canonical Common Sense Majority mission statement is:

> I think most people who currently identify as left-wing or right-wing are basically normal sane people with common-sense views who have more in common with each other than our extremely-polarized political/cultural environment implies, and I want to see this quiet middle majority recognize itself as a majority and gain more influence.

This is the statement CSM uses as the root cause for "stuff aligned with the CSM mission statement" in Alignment and as the central statement users can view or sign in Tally.
