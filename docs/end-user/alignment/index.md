# Alignment

Alignment is the cause-funding UI for ongoing public-good funding. Use it to explore causes, review funding portals, and pledge through trusted alignment judgments.

## What can I do here?

- **[Pledge funds to a cause](pledge-to-a-cause.md)** — Pledge monthly, delegate to someone you trust, and let them decide where it goes. Revocable anytime.
- **[Become a delegate](become-a-delegate.md)** — Direct others' funds toward projects you believe in. Build a public track record.
- **[Help connect things](help-connect-things.md)** — Vouch that projects and content align with causes. Your influence grows with followers.
