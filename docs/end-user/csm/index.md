# Common Sense Majority

Common Sense Majority (CSM) is a movement for revealing and organizing the hidden common ground that already exists across political divides.

The idea is simple. Most people are more reasonable than the political system makes them look. On issue after issue, a quiet supermajority holds a sensible position — but it's invisible, because the system is organized around two coalitions dominated by their loudest members. CSM makes that majority visible: people say what they actually think, an AI mediator finds where the two sides already overlap, and running tallies show just how many people agree.

If you're someone who's tired of the anger and wants to see more bridge-building between the two sides, you're exactly who this is for.

## How it works, in one breath

- People sign statements in their own words saying what they actually believe.
- An AI **mediator** — opinionated but fully transparent — reads what moderate people on both sides wrote, notices where they don't really conflict, and suggests bridge statements each side can sign without betraying their tribe.
- **Running tallies** count direct *and* indirect supporters, turning scattered private agreement into a visible, countable majority.
- **Crowdfunded noninflammatory content** is how those bridge statements actually reach people — written by anyone, on the platforms they already use.

No organization holds the money (it flows onchain), the AI's prompts are open for anyone to read, and you choose who you trust at every step. That combination is what lets a *cross-partisan* movement be trusted by both sides at once.

## How CSM uses the Commonality ecosystem

CSM isn't a standalone app — it's a movement built on the [Commonality](../commonality/vision-and-strategy/README.md) platform, which it uses without being the same thing as it:

- **Civility** funds bridge-building media that lets people hear a strong argument without feeling despised.
- **Tally** lets people sign movement-aligned statements in their own words and see direct plus indirect support.
- **Alignment** provides ongoing cause funding and funding portals.
- **LazyGiving** provides one-off project funding for organizing projects.
- **Conceptspace** provides the underlying statement, implication, attester, and trust primitives.
